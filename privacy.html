PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Ciphera Daily ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

01 — INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you create an account and use Ciphera Daily, we collect:

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Display name — the agent codename you choose, shown on the leaderboard.
  • User ID — a unique identifier assigned to your account used to store your puzzle history and leaderboard scores.

02 — HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Your information is used solely to operate the app:

  • Authenticating you when you sign in.
  • Saving your puzzle completion history and scores.
  • Displaying your name on the global leaderboard.
  • Sending optional daily reminder notifications (only if you enable them).

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any third-party marketing purposes.

03 — DATA STORAGE

Account data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party backend service. Supabase stores data on servers in the United States. You can review Supabase's privacy practices at supabase.com/privacy.

Puzzle progress and preferences are also stored locally on your device using Apple's standard local storage (SwiftData / UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device unless synced through your account.

04 — DATA SHARING

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties except as required to operate the service (Supabase, described above) or as required by law.

Your display name and leaderboard score are visible to other users of the app by design. Your email address is never shared publicly.

05 — TRACKING

Ciphera Daily does not track you across other apps or websites. We do not use advertising networks, behavioral analytics SDKs, or fingerprinting tools of any kind.

06 — NOTIFICATIONS

If you enable daily reminders, the app will schedule a local notification on your device at your chosen time. No notification data is transmitted to our servers. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings app.

07 — CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Ciphera Daily requires account creation and is rated 13+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

08 — YOUR RIGHTS

You may at any time:

  • Update your display name within the app.
  • Delete your account by contacting us — this will permanently remove all data associated with your account.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

09 — CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
